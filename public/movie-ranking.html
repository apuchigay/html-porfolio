My top 3 movies of all time

The Godfather

Best movie of all time, a masterpiece of cinema that explores the complexities of family, power, and loyalty. The Godfather is a timeless classic that has left an indelible mark on film history.

Grave of the fireflies

A heartbreaking and poignant tale of two siblings struggling to survive in post-war Japan. Grave of the Fireflies is a powerful reminder of the devastating impact of war on innocent lives, and it remains one of the most emotionally impactful animated films ever made.

Oppenheimer

A gripping and thought-provoking biographical drama that delves into the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the father of the atomic bomb. Oppenheimer is a masterful exploration of the moral dilemmas faced by scientists and the profound consequences of their discoveries.
